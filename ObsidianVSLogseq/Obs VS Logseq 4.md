
# Why Obsidian Wins Over Logseq — Performance and Scale

Obsidian handles large vaults efficiently.
Thousands of files remain searchable and responsive.
External indexing tools can enhance performance further.
Markdown files load independently when opened.

Logseq sometimes slows with heavy block graphs.

Obsidian keeps complexity at the file level.
This reduces overhead during navigation.
Large knowledge bases remain practical.
Scaling favors simplicity.
